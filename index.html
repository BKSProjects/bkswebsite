Hi, I'm Bailey.

Bailey is a costume maker and special effects artist by trade. Beginning her journey into theatre and movies in California where she learned SFX make-up at the renown Cinema Make-up School in LA and studied theatre arts at University of San Diego. Bailey has gained experience interning and working on both theatre productions and student films for several years. Moving to New Zealand to study at the prestigious Toi Whakaari, graduated with a diploma in Costume Construction. Bailey is a hard-working, professional woman who is always looking to further extend her knowledge in costuming and SFX make-up.
